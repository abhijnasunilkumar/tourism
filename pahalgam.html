Explore Pahalgam

Pahalgam is a picturesque hill station known for its stunning landscapes, ancient temples, and the famous Betaab River, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and spiritual seekers.

Lidder Valley

Lidder Valley is a serene valley in Pahalgam, known for its crystal-clear rivers, dense forests, and breathtaking mountain vistas.

Mt. Kolahoi

Mt. Kolahoi is a majestic mountain in Pahalgam, known for its snow-capped peaks, diverse wildlife, and challenging trekking routes.

Betaab Valley

Betaab Valley is a scenic valley in Pahalgam, known for its lush greenery, pristine environment, and the famous Betaab River flowing through it.

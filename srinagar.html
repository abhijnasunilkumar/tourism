Explore Srinagar

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Srinagar through its famous lakes, gardens, historical monuments and colourful landscapes.

Dal Lake

Dal Lake is Srinagar's most famous attraction, known for peaceful Shikara rides, floating markets and beautiful houseboats.

Pari Mahal

Explore the historic Palace of Fairies overlooking Dal Lake with breathtaking panoramic views of Srinagar.

Tulip Garden

Asia's largest tulip garden blooms every spring with millions of colourful flowers creating a magical experience.
